InReach Commands

Available commands for satellite messages.

Weather forecast

weather or vær - Today's forecast

weather detailed - Hourly encoded forecast

weather tomorrow - Tomorrow's forecast

Avalanche warning

avalanche or skred - Encoded avalanche data for your position

Shelter (nearest cabins)

shelter - Find the 4 nearest cabins/huts from your GPS position

Hiking route

route 61.62,8.63 - Route to coordinates

route 2 - Route to cabin #2 from last shelter result

Locate (MapShare)

locate BEAMC - Get latest position from a Garmin MapShare feed

ChatGPT (general questions)

Any message that doesn't match a command is sent to ChatGPT. Conversation history is kept for 60 minutes.

Train departures

train Oslo S - Bergen - Next departures

train location - Lillehammer - From nearest station

train stationboard Hønefoss - Departure board
